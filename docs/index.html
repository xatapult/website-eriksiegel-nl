With components and wires clearly visible, installations are created that are meant to evoke amazement at the usually hidden complexity of technology.

Older electromechanical parts, whose operation is clearly visible and understandable, are considered especially beautiful.

By combining these with modern components, such as LEDs, micro-controllers and semiconductors, together with moving parts, such as marble tracks and electric motors, interesting objects are created.
