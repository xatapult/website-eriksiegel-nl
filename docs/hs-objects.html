 The Henk Slomp objects 

Henk Slomp is a Dutch artist that works with steel and other cool materials. He lives and has a gallery at Rue Porte Fouron 14, Lormes, France (in the Morvan).

We met in the summer of 2021, purely by chance. Together we created an installation with steel egg-shaped objects hanging in a space. These objects click, light, move and react on where you are in the room.

You can visit the installation (the gallery is open on Thursdays and Saturdays), walk between the objects and see and hear how they react to you. It’s a magical experience.
