 The incredible inside-out pinball machine 

Once upon a time I bought a derelict pinball machine.

According to the previous owner it was "beyond repair"...

I was able to get my hands on the original electric schematic, so I started restoring this beautiful piece of machinery.

End result after more than two years: the pinball machine was fully functioning again!

This is how it looks now.

It was too big to keep. I gave it to a friend who has a large man-cave.
